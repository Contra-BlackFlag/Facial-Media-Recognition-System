How to use FMRS

  1. Click UPLOAD to add photos to the system's library. The more photos you add, the better the results.
  2. Click RETRIEVE to open your camera and take a selfie.
  3. The system will find all photos in the library that contain your face.
  4. Once images appear, click Select to download your favorites.
